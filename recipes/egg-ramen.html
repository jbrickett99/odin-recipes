Egg Ramen

Description

Let's face it. It's late. You should probably be in bed. You might be a little tipsy. But you want a snack.

In times like these, Ramen fails to disappoint. ESPECIALLY when you cook it right. Keep reading.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Maruchan Ramen (chicken flavor)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Crack and beat one egg in a bowl.
  2. Before opening ramen package, crush noodles inside the package.
  3. Pour crushed ramen noodles into bowl with egg.
  4. Fill bowl with water, just enough to cover the noodles.
  5. Cook in microwave for 2 and a half minutes.
  6. Carefully add the slice of cheese and the chicken seasoning to the bowl and mix thoroughly.
  7. Cook for another 30 seconds.
  8. Immediately try to eat the ramen and scald your tongue.
  9. Wait 3 minutes for ramen to cool enough to eat.
  10. Let your late night munchies be satisfied.
