Description

This receipe is an American classic, enhanced by the rich, smokey flavor only a Trager grill can provide.

This recipe can be modified depending on the level of smokey-ness you prefer. For a more smokey flavor, decrease temperature and increase cooking time. For a milder smokey taste, increase temperature and decrease cooking time. Burgers are done when they reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees Farenheit.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger meat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Any other condiments you desire

Steps

  1. Load grill with wood pellets. Signature pellets are recommended, but others may be used.
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  3. Place patties on grill
  4. Lower temperature to 300 degrees
  5. Cook until burgers reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees
